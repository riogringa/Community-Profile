Deportations: A Lack of Discretion

In 2011, the Immigration and Customs Enforcement director issued a memo on prosecutorial discretion, guiding the agency to take into account undocumented immigrants' family and community ties during enforcement. But from October 2012 through Dec. 31, 2014, immigration courts used this policy in a minority of deportation cases.
